Jar Ball Stack

Instructions

The game has 3 jars and colored number balls. The goal is to stack the balls into jars across 3 levels:

1.Ball to Jar: Click a ball, then select a jar to move it there.

2.Jar to Jar: Click a jar, then click another jar to move the top ball.

3.Remove Ball: Click any ball in a jar to remove it.
